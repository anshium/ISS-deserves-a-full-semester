Shantanu Mukherjee, popularly known as Shaan, is an Indian playback singer, live performer, composer, actor and television host. He has recorded numerous songs for films and albums in various Indian languages and has established himself as one of the best and highest paid playback singers of Indian Cinema. Born on 30 September 1972 in Mumbai in a Bengali family, his grandfather, Jahar Mukherjee was a well known lyricist, and his father late Manas Mukherjee, was a music director. His sister Sagarika is a singer as well.
