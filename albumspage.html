Shantanu Mukherjee (born 30 September 1972), popularly known as Shaan, is an Indian playback singer, composer, actor and television host.

He has recorded numerous songs for films and albums in various Indian languages. Shaan hosted the shows Sa Re Ga Ma Pa, Sa Re Ga Ma Pa L'il Champs, Star Voice of India and STAR Voice of India 2.
